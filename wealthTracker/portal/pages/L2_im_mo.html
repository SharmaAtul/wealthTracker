Monitoring

 

What is Monitoring?

A risk management technique in relation to investments in which a professional reviews the clients portfolio and investment on an active and ongoing basis.

There are a number of monitoring services available going from passive to active monitoring.

Active monitoring involves reviewing investments and making adjustments on a daily basis if required and because of the daily commitment is expensive and only worthwhile for larger portfolios. However some research houses and fund managers provide a monitoring service for a set portfolio

Who will benefit from monitoring?

Clients that wish to reduce the risk of underperformance and want to make sure that their financial planning takes into consideration the latest financial and legislative news.
