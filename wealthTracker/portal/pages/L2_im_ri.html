What is responsible investment exposure?

Responsible investment is an umbrella term to describe an investment process which takes environmental, social, governance (ES G) or ethical considerations into account. This process stands in addition to, or is incorporated into the usual fundamental investment selection and management process. This involves the inclusion of one or more of the following practices in the research, analysis, selection and monitoring of an investment.

Responsible investment is a way to generate competitive returns and find sustainable solutions to many of the challenges we face in the 21st century
