Investment Management

Investment management is the professional management of various securities and assets in order to meet specified investment goals for the benefit of investors.

Securities and assets are generally divided into the following main asset classes:

  1. 1. Cash
  2. 2. Fixed Interest
  3. 3. Property
  4. 4. Australian Shares
  5. 5. International Shares

The provision of 'investment management services' includes elements of financial statement analysis, asset selection, stock selection, plan implementation and ongoing monitoring of investments.
