Portfolio Administration

Portfolio administration can be divided into investment administration and portfolio management and includes the gathering, processing, displaying, and reporting of a client’s portfolio.

Depending on the level of administration required these services range from displaying a  simple account balance from one investment or bank account to a full range of asset classes and securities from a large number of investment and securities providers.

Sometimes the service includes an asset allocation function and a current and proposed scenario function, real time financial news, up to date portfolio access and detailed taxation reporting.
